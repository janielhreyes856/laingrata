Our story

A culinary romance, written in Camden.

Two chefs grinding through long hours under the same roof, united by passion. Karla carried the flavors of her mother's and grandmother's cooking; Ernesto brought years of experience and deep Camden roots.

During the pandemic they sold bread to keep afloat. That spirit of resilience became La Ingrata — a place to share their culture with the community they call home.
